## Evaluation Criteria for RAG Performance

### Quality Indicators:
- **Accuracy**: Factually correct information
- **Completeness**: Comprehensive coverage of relevant aspects
- **Relevance**: Information directly addresses the query
- **Coherence**: Logical flow and clear organization
- **Source Attribution**: Proper citation of document sections
- **Nuance**: Recognition of complexity and multiple perspectives

### Red Flags:
- **Hallucination**: Information not present in the source document
- **Contradiction**: Conflicting statements within the response
- **Incomplete Retrieval**: Missing relevant information that exists in the document
- **Poor Integration**: Disjointed information from multiple sources
- **Bias Amplification**: Reinforcing problematic perspectives without acknowledgment
- **Overconfidence**: Definitive statements about uncertain or speculative topics

Use these queries progressively to test your RAG system's capabilities and identify areas for improvement in retrieval accuracy, reasoning depth, and response quality.
